1.1. Cookie Policy Introduction:

Begin your Privacy Policy with a brief introduction outlining the purpose and content of the policy, so users understand its main points.

2. What Are Cookies:

Provide a clear and concise definition of what cookies are. Explain that they are small text files stored on a user's device when they visit your website.

3. Types of Cookies:

Describe the different types of cookies you use on your website. Common categories include: Essential Cookies: Explain that these are necessary for the website to function properly (e.g., for user authentication or shopping cart functionality). Analytics Cookies: Mention that these collect anonymous data about website usage to help improve the user experience. Marketing Cookies: If applicable, state that these are used for marketing and advertising purposes.

4. Purpose of Cookies:

Detail the specific purposes for which each type of cookie is used. For example, explain that analytics cookies are used to track website traffic and behavior, while marketing cookies may be used for targeted advertising.

5. Cookie Duration:

Clarify how long each type of cookie will remain on the user's device. Some cookies expire when the user closes their browser (session cookies), while others may have a longer lifespan (persistent cookies).
